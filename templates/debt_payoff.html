💡 Tips for Success

❄️ Snowball

  • Best for motivation
  • Quick wins build momentum
  • See debts disappear fast
  • Good if you struggle with motivation

🏔️ Avalanche

  • Saves the most money
  • Mathematically optimal
  • Lower total interest paid
  • Gets debt-free faster

💰 General Tips

  • Always pay minimums on all debts
  • Any extra money goes to target debt
  • Don't add new debt
  • Even small extra payments help!
